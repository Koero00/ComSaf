# Community Safety App  

## Overview  
The **Community Safety App** is designed to provide faster assistance to people with medical issues by connecting them with first-aid certified individuals, emergency contacts, and first responders.  

## How It Works  
1. **Sign Up** – Users select their role, medical condition, and emergency contacts.  
2. **Setup** – Configure preferred emergency settings.  
3. **Help Request:**  
   - **Minor Emergency** – Alerts emergency contacts and updates location.  
   - **Major Emergency** – Notifies medical emergency services and nearby first-aid trained users.  
4. **Assistance Arrives** – Helpers provide support while emergency services respond.  
5. **Resolution** – Users update their help status, and helpers gain recognition.  

## App Structure  
- **home.dart** – Main interface for users.  
- **topbar.dart** – Navigation bar for seamless access.
